# Admission Requirements

## General Requirements (for Senior High School Graduates AY 2026–2027)

| Requirement | Required upon enrollment | Promissory Note accepted |
|-------------|--------------------------|--------------------------|
| SAR Form 1, Route and Approval Slip, Health Information Form | YES |  |
| **For Graduates of Senior High School AY 2026–2027** |  |  |
| Duly signed Waiver/Certification/Undertaking | YES | NO |
| Original High School Card (F138/Grade 12 Card) issued by Principal or Registrar (with school dry seal) | YES | NO |
| Original or certified true copy of Grade 10 and 11 card (with school dry seal) | YES | NO |
| Notarized certification of non-enrollment for high school/senior high school graduates who have not enrolled in any technical/diploma/degree program immediately after graduation with “waiver” that if there is concealment of previous enrolment, PUP enrolment becomes null and void | YES | NO |
| Original PSA Birth Certificate | YES | YES |
| Certification of Good Moral Character issued by Principal or Guidance Counselor with school dry seal (Date on/after Graduation) | YES | YES |
| Current Medical Certificate from any accredited healthcare facilities with letterhead signed by a Medical Doctor (printed name, signature & License Number) | YES | NO |
| Chest X-ray result (Validity of the Chest X-ray result should be no more than 6 months from the scheduled enrollment) | YES | NO |
| Duly accomplished Health Information Form for Students | YES | NO |
| Three (3) pcs 2”x2” colored picture with white background and the student's name on the photo | YES | YES |
| One (1) pc. long brown envelope | YES | NO |
| For person with disability: PWD ID or PWD certification issued by the local government unit | YES | YES |
| PUPCET e-Permit (Optional) | NO | YES |

---

## For Previous Senior High School Graduates / High School Graduates of Past School Year(s)

*In addition to the above admission requirements:*

| Requirement | Required upon enrollment | Promissory Note accepted |
|-------------|--------------------------|--------------------------|
| Certification from the respective senior high school registrar with school dry seal and noted by the Principal that no copy of F137-A has been sent to other college or university | YES | NO |
