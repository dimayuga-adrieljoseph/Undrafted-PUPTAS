# **Discussion Proper**

## **1\. Document Evaluation Process Updates**

The current document evaluation workflow shall be revised to provide a more streamlined process for applicants requiring further assistance.

### **A. Document Evaluator View**

The existing **"Require Promissory Note"** action shall be replaced with:

**"Require Applicant to Go to Guidance Office."**

This action will be used whenever an applicant needs additional document verification or assistance before continuing the enrollment process.

### **B. Applicant View**

The applicant interface shall be updated with the following changes:

* Display the message:  
  * **"Please go to the Guidance Office."**  
* Display an action notice:  
  * **"Action Required: Go to Guidance Office."**  
* Remove the **Returned** status.  
* Applicants shall remain under **In Progress** status even if their documents require Guidance Office processing.  
* Remove the **Resubmit Documents** option from the applicant portal.

These changes ensure that applicants are directly guided to the appropriate office instead of resubmitting documents through the system.

## **2\. Guidance Office Workflow**

Applicants referred from Document Evaluation shall proceed to the Guidance Office.

The following process was agreed upon:

1. The applicant reports to the Guidance Office.  
2. A designated Guidance Assistant reviews the applicant's concern.  
3. The Guidance Assistant receives and pass the applicant  
4. The applicant continues the enrollment process after Guidance Office processing.

This workflow centralizes document handling and minimizes unnecessary applicant actions.

## **3\. Grade Evaluation Process Updates**

Additional functionality shall be added to the Grade Evaluation module.

### **A. Grade Evaluator View**

For applicants with grade-related concerns or discrepancies, a new action shall be added:

**"Require Applicant to Go to Admissions Office."**

This allows Grade Evaluators to formally refer applicants for further grade verification or correction.

### **B. Applicant View**

The following behavior shall be implemented:

* The **Resubmit Grades** option shall remain available.  
* Applicants shall continue to appear under the **Grade Evaluator** stage.  
* The applicant status shall remain **In Progress** until the grade evaluation process is completed.

This allows applicants to submit corrected grade information without restarting the evaluation workflow.

## **4\. Interview Module Enhancements**

Several enhancements were proposed to improve interview monitoring and information accessibility.

### **Additional Reports**

The Interview module shall include the following reference reports:

* Grade Evaluator Reports  
* Document Evaluator Reports

The template will be sent by Maam Dianne

### **Interview Start Time**

A new feature shall be added to record the time an applicant begins the interview.

Implementation options include:

* A manual input field for start time or  
* A button that automatically records the current system time when the applicant is seated.  
* Also End Time

This feature will assist in monitoring interview duration and overall applicant processing time.

# **Summary of Agreed Updates**

### **Document Evaluation**

* Replace **Require Promissory Note** with **Require Applicant to Go to Guidance Office**.  
* Display **"Please go to the Guidance Office"** and **"Action Required: Go to Guidance Office"** messages on the applicant portal.  
* Remove the **Returned** status and keep the applicant status as **In Progress** while under Guidance Office processing.  
* Remove the **Resubmit Documents** option from the applicant portal.

### **Guidance Office**

* Applicants referred from Document Evaluation shall proceed to the Guidance Office.  
* A designated Guidance Assistant shall receive, assist, and endorse the applicant for the next stage of the enrollment process.

### **Grade Evaluation**

* Add **Require Applicant to Go to Admissions Office** as a rejection action in the Grade Evaluator view.  
* Retain the **Resubmit Grades** option for applicants.  
* Keep applicants under the **Grade Evaluator** stage with an **In Progress** status until grade concerns are resolved.

### **Interview Module**

* Add **Grade Evaluator Reports** and **Document Evaluator Reports** for interviewer reference.  
* The report template shall be provided by **Ma'am Dianne**.  
* Add **Interview Start Time** and **Interview End Time** fields, either through manual input or automatic time-recording buttons, to monitor interview duration.
